Some Famous Hotels in Egypt

Steigenberger Nile Palace

  • Situated in Luxor, Egypt, directly on the banks of the Nile River, offering stunning river and mountain views.
  • Close to major historical sites like Luxor Temple, Karnak Temple, and the Valley of the Kings, which is about 26 km across the river.
  • Approximately 10 km from Luxor International Airport, making it easily accessible for travelers.
