Some Famous Companies In Egypt

Orascom Construction PLC

  • Overview: OC is a leading construction and engineering company with a focus on infrastructure, industrial, and civil projects in Egypt and the broader Middle East and North Africa region. It has a history tied to Orascom Construction Industries (OCI), which split into OC and OCI N.V. in 2015.

Egyptian General Petroleum Corporation

  • What it is: EGPC is a state-owned Egyptian government entity that oversees and coordinates petroleum exploration, production, refining, and distribution activities in Egypt. It acts as a central planning and supervisory body for the oil and gas sector on behalf of the Egyptian state.
