My favirout places to see in Egypt

The Pyramids

The Giza pyramid complex is on the Giza Plateau near Cairo, Egypt. It includes three main pyramids: the Great Pyramid (Pyramid of Khufu), the Pyramid of Khafre, and the Pyramid of Menkaure, plus a collection of smaller pyramids, causeways, temples, and the Great Sphinx.

Sheikh Zayed

It is Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan Founding father of the UAE. Longtime ruler of Abu Dhabi (1966–2004) and the first President of the UAE (1971–2004). Played a pivotal role in uniting the emirates into one federation.
